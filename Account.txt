**Account**:Account Represents general ledger accounts used for classifying financial activity, including 'Bank', 'Accounts Receivable', 'Accounts Payable', 'Fixed Asset', 'Expense', 'Income', 'Equity', 'Cost of Goods Sold', 'Credit Card', 'Deferred Revenue'. Accounts are fundamental to the General Ledger (GL) and are used to track financial transactions in NetSuite.
